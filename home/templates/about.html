About Samsara

The Samsara Story

Where ancient wisdom meets modern streets. A cycle of rebirth, told through fabric.

The philosophy

What is Samsara?

Samsara is an ancient concept describing the endless cycle of birth, death, and rebirth. It is the wheel of life that keeps turning, forever evolving, forever transforming.

We chose this name because it represents who we are: a diaspora brand caught between two worlds, constantly reinventing ourselves while staying rooted in our heritage. Every collection is a rebirth. Every design tells a story. Every garment carries the weight of generations.

The mission

Bridging Two Worlds

We are not trying to recreate traditional Indian wear. We are not diluting our heritage to fit in. We are doing something different: merging the bold aesthetics of modern streetwear with the depth and spirituality of Indian culture.

Drop shoulders. Boxy fits. Heavyweight cottons. Clean lines. These are the tools of contemporary street fashion, but the stories woven into them are timeless.

Design language

Our Design Language

Minimalist

Clean lines and uncluttered aesthetics. We let the silhouette speak before the graphics do.

Earthy

Deep stone tones with muted orange accents: grounded colors that feel timeless and rooted.

Story-Driven

Every piece carries meaning, whether through subtle motifs or overt cultural references.

The journey

Designed in the Diaspora

Samsara was born out of a feeling familiar to many second-generation immigrants: being caught between two identities. Not quite here, not quite there. Too American for India, too Indian for America.

Instead of seeing this as a weakness, we embraced it. Our duality became our strength. We started designing clothes that reflected this experience: modern enough for the streets, meaningful enough to carry our heritage forward.

This is streetwear for the in-between. For those who code-switch effortlessly. For those whose playlists jump from Bollywood to hip-hop. For those who grew up on chai and coffee, on dosas and pizza, on tradition and rebellion.

We are not just building a brand. We are building a home for people who have always felt like they existed in between worlds.

What is next

The Cycle Continues

Drop 001 is just the beginning. We are building a universe where every collection explores a different aspect of Indian culture: mythology, philosophy, festivals, folklore. Each drop tells a new story and pushes the boundaries of what Indian streetwear can be.

We are not interested in fast fashion or trend-chasing. Samsara is about creating pieces that last, that mean something, and that stay in your rotation for years.
